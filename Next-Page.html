Ingredients Measurement
rice 8 cup
Vegetable oil 250 ml
Margarine 100g
Chicken/turkey or beef 500g or 1kg
Green pepper 1 big size
Grinded white/dry pepper 1-2 tablespoons
Green deans (diced) 1 cup
Carrot 1 tin
Green peas 1 cups
Liver of beef (cooked and diced) 2 cups
Sweet corn 1 tablespoon
Thyme 1 tablespoon
Maggi to taste
Salt to taste
Fried rice spice (optional) to taste
Mixed spice 2 tablespoons
Spring onoins 1 to 2 stalks
Turmeric or curry 2 tablespoons
Runner beans 100g

Methods of Preparation:

  1. Parboil the rice to tender, wash and set aside.
  2. Prepare the vegtables and set aside.
  3. Wash your chicken,liver of beef or kidney with water and salt very well. spice with curry,thyme,maggi,salt,onoins and steam for 2 minutes.You can season your chicken, keep and allow to mix with spice well before steaming and cooking
  4. Season your chicken or meat stock, add greener (original fried rice spice)
    5. or turmeric. Finish the cooking of the rice with the stock then remove from the heat and set aside
  5. In a sauce pan, add your vegetable oil, butter and heat for some seconds. Then add onions
    6. and garlic and stir. Pour in the liver already cooked and diced. Add all the vegtables. stir for 2 minutes, pour in the cooked rice and mix together cook till done. Finally, add sweet corn and turn properly.
  6. Your fried rice is ready to serve. Serve with fried chicken or beef
