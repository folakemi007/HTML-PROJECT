My Favourite Meal

Fried Rice.

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usuall mixed with other ingredients

such has vegetables, seafood or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Fried rice first developed during the Sui Dynasty in China and as such all fried rice dishes can trace their origins to Chinese fried Rice.

Benefits of Fried Rice:

This dish is full of healthy fiber, protein, good fat, and good carbs, and is loaded with vitamins, minerals and antioxidants, fried rice a good source of Vitamin E.
